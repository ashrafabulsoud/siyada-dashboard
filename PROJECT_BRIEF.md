# SiyadaTech — Executive Dashboard: Project Brief

## Company Overview

SiyadaTech is a Saudi-born multi-engine AI company targeting a compressed 5-year path to IPO on the Tadawul. The company operates three interdependent revenue engines:

- **Engine 1 — Lenci:** A product-led growth AI visual tool targeting e-commerce owners. Dual acquisition channels: Agency B2B (50 early adopters) and PLG via a "Magic Page" funnel.
- **Engine 2 — AI Services:** Cash flow and enterprise trust engine. SiyadaTech is the exclusive AI arm of Al Nafidha, a 30-year IT integrator with 7,000+ active clients.
- **Engine 3 — Partnerships:** Scale and recurring revenue engine. First deal: Floaino (1,000 licenses closing), using a repeatable playbook: Identify Vendor → Sign Reseller Deal → Localize & Comply → Deploy via Al Nafidha → Collect Margin.

- Floaino partnership: 1,000 licenses, closing.
- Team: War room ready — 6 AI Engineers, 1 Data Engineer, 1 PM, CTO, CEO, BD, Sales, Marketing + Tunisia surge team.

## Dashboard Purpose

This is a **living executive dashboard** designed for the board of directors and C-level leadership of SiyadaTech. It must:

1. Answer at a glance: "Where are we, what are we doing, and where are we going?"
2. Track the health of all three revenue engines simultaneously.
3. Show progress against the 90-day sprint plan and the 5-year IPO roadmap.
4. Surface risks and blockers without requiring a meeting.
5. Be updated regularly by the operations team — this is not a one-time presentation.

## Audience

- **Board of Directors**: Strategic view, financial trajectory, phase progress, major risks.
- **C-Level (CEO, CTO, BD)**: Engine status, KPI performance, 90-day sprint execution.

## Design Direction

The dashboard must match SiyadaTech's brand identity:
- **Color palette**: Deep navy (#0B1929), gold/amber accent (#D4A853), white text, muted slate for secondary elements.
- **Tone**: Confident, premium, execution-focused. Not corporate-generic. The War Plan documents use a dark-background, gold-accent visual language — the dashboard should feel like an extension of that identity.
- **Language**: English primary. All labels and metrics in English.
